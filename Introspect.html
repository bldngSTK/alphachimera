Introspect


Broader topics have much more content and answerable prompts, so they take more time, but often yield the greatest wide-range results that can affect multiple dimensions at once.
- Great for people who have plenty of time and are willing to have a comprehensive understanding of a topic

Broader Topics:

 Understanding Worry Detaching from Sadness How to Do Things


Specific topics are great for having a focused assessment and solution process, but oftentimes don't benefit any dimensions other than what they claim to help with.
- Great for people who are not willing to put forth much time and only want the solution-oriented information.

Specific Topics:

 Emotional Cleansing Manage Anger Cultivate Productivity Fixing Unsolvable Problems Mitigate Burnout


Writing essays to reach a word count feels the most tedious and pointless, but can lead to the greatest long-term revelations (the first couple words may be reflexively simple inputs of the mind, but the more we write, the more we're forced to truly put down what we're actually thinking. Once we know what we truly believe deep down, then we can make focused change.)
- Great for people who have plenty of time and are interested in understanding what they authentically believe deep-down.

Essay Topics:

 Discover Life Purpose Outweigh Addiction Burdenlessly Socialize Passive Journaling Draining Desires
