Draining Desires


If you had a time machine that could take you to the past, what unsatisfied desires would you wish to satisfy?

Our unsatisfied desires can drain our ability to complete tasks because our mind constantly spends some energy remembering to satisfy it.
Write 500 words about various unfulfilled desires throughout your entire life that have yet to be satisfied (from the present or past.)
